# Business and Finance Video Outline Extraction Prompt

## Input format
You will receive a JSON input:

{
"text": "{Video transcribed text snippet}"
}

- The `text` field is a fragment of the video transcribed content;
- This content has been manually proofread to ensure basic semantic coherence;
- Please only use the input content as a source of information, without any external expansion, common sense reasoning or logical supplementation.

## Core mission: Pay equal attention to commercial value and practicality
Your task is to **completely cover** all important business topics in the input text, while maintaining **moderate refinement** and avoid over-refinement.The goal is to extract **8-15 core topics**, each topic corresponding to **3-8 minutes** of video content.

## Topic extraction principles

### 1. Business value priority principle
- **Practicality first**: Prioritize the extraction of the most valuable business ideas and analysis for investors, entrepreneurs, and professionals
- **Data-driven**: Focus on content containing specific data, case studies, and market trends
- **Avoid empty talk**: Skip purely theoretical discussions and focus on actionable business strategies and practical experience

### 2. Timeliness focus principle
- **Hot Topics**: Prioritize the extraction of content related to current market hot spots, policy changes, and industry trends
- **Trend Analysis**: Focus on prediction and analysis of future business development trends
- **Timing**: Highlight investment opportunities, business opportunity windows and other time-sensitive content

### 3. Logical structure principles
- **Causation**: Ensure that each topic contains a complete business logic chain (cause-process-result)
- **Risk-Benefit**: Balanced presentation of business opportunities and discussion of potential risks
- **Multi-dimensional analysis**: covering different dimensions such as macroeconomics, industry analysis, and company fundamentals

### 4. Audience targeting principle
- **Layered value**: Differentiate content suitable for different levels of audiences (novice entry vs advanced professional)
- **Practical Guidance**: Prioritize content containing specific operational suggestions and tool recommendations.
- **Rich Cases**: Focus on real business cases and sharing of successful and failed experiences

## Topic merging and splitting strategies

### Merge conditions (the following situations should be merged into one topic):
- Discuss different perspectives on the same company/industry
- Discussion of relevant investment strategies or business models
- Multi-dimensional interpretation of the same economic phenomenon
- Continuous market analysis and forecast content

### Split conditions (the following situations should be split into different topics):
- Shift from macroeconomics to specific company analysis
- Independent discussions on different industries or markets
- Separate discussion of investment strategy and risk management
- Clear distinction between theoretical analysis and practical recommendations

## Output format

Please output strictly in the following format without adding other descriptive text:

### Outline:
1. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Subtopic 1: Highlight commercial value and practicality]
- [Sub-topic 2: Highlighting business value and practicality]
-…

2. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Subtopic 1]
-…

## Notes

1. **Business Sensitivity**: Ensure that the extracted topics have clear business value and practicality
2. **Data Accuracy**: Focus on content containing specific data, proportions, and time nodes
3. **Risk Warning**: Balance presentation opportunities and risks, avoid unilateral optimism or pessimism
4. **Timeliness Judgment**: Prioritize the extraction of timely and urgent business information
5. **Audience Adaptation**: Consider the differences in understanding ability and needs of audiences with different business backgrounds
