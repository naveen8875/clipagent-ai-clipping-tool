#Business and Finance Video Time Point Positioning Prompt

## Core principles: accuracy, completeness, clear business logic

1. **Precise positioning**: The timestamp must strictly correspond to the core discussion content related to business topics.
2. **Completeness**: The time range must completely cover all key argument points of a business topic to ensure that the business logic is complete.
3. **Natural Boundaries**: Prioritize locating time points at business point of view transitions, case switches, or logical paragraph boundaries.

---

## Key instructions: how to determine `start_time` and `end_time`

### Determination of `start_time`:
- Should be positioned at the start time of the **first sentence core point** of the discussion of the business topic.
- Ignore any extraneous pleasantries, disclaimers, or transitions that precede the point.
- **Preferred**: Start at the boundary of business logic to ensure that the topic is introduced naturally and logically.
- **Special considerations for commercial content**: If there is important background or data reference, the starting time should be moved forward appropriately.

### Determination of `end_time` (most important):
- **Must** be the end timestamp** of the **last sentence** covering the core discussion of this business topic.
- **Business Logic Integrity**: Ensure that core content such as business analysis, investment recommendations, and risk warnings are complete without logical gaps.
- **Natural ending**: It is preferred to end at a concluding sentence, conclusion of investment advice or topic change.
- **RISK INCLUSION**: If there is an important risk warning or disclaimer at the end of the topic, it should be included.
- If the discussion of the business topic continues until the end of the provided SRT text block, then `end_time` should be the end timestamp of the last relevant subtitle.
- **Wrong practice**: Set `end_time` to the end time of the entire text block without thinking.This is absolutely to be avoided.

### Principles of commercial content duration control:
- **Target Duration**: Each business topic segment should be between 3-8 minutes
- **Complex Analysis Duration**: Complex business topics involving multi-dimensional analysis can be appropriately extended to 10 minutes
- **News content**: Market news, policy interpretations, etc. can be compressed to 2-3 minutes
- **In-depth analysis**: In-depth content such as industry research and company analysis can be extended to 12 minutes

### Special treatment for commercial content:
- **Data-intensive paragraphs**: Paragraphs containing a large amount of financial data and market data should be kept intact
- **Case Analysis**: A complete business case analysis should be taken as a whole to avoid splitting
- **Investment Suggestions**: Investment strategies and risk warnings should appear together and cannot be separated.
- **Policy Interpretation**: Policy content and impact analysis should maintain integrity

---

## Input format
You will receive a JSON object containing:
- `outline`: A JSON array containing **multiple** business topics to be processed.
- `srt_text`: A **single** SRT text block related to this batch of topics, in the format of `serial number\nstart --> end\ntext\n\n`.

## Output format
- Strictly follow the structure of the input outline and add `start_time` and `end_time` fields to **each** topic object.
- **Key:** On output, rename the input's `title` field to `outline` and the `subtopics` field to `content`.
- The final output is a JSON array containing **all** processed topics.
- Make sure the time format is `HH:MM:SS,mmm`.

**Strict JSON output requirements:**
1. The output must start with `[` and end with `]`. Do not add any explanation text, title or Markdown code block mark
2. Use standard English double quotation marks ", never use Chinese quotation marks "" or single quotation marks
3. Make sure that all parentheses and square brackets match correctly, objects are separated by commas, and there is no comma after the last object.
4. Quotes in strings must be escaped as \"
5. Cannot contain any comments, extra text or control characters
6. Ensure that the JSON format is fully valid and can be parsed by standard JSON parsers

## Commercial content time positioning example

### Correct time positioning:
- **Investment Strategy Discussion**: Start with "I think the current market..." and end with "This is my investment advice"
- **Company Analysis**: Starting from "Let's take a look at this company..." and ending with "Comprehensive Assessment Results"
- **Industry Trends**: Starting from "The development trend of this industry..." and ending with "Future Outlook"

### Mistakes to avoid:
- Disconnect in the middle of financial data explanation
- Separate investment advice and risk warnings
- Truncate at key argument points in the case analysis
- Omit important market background information

## Notes

1. **Business Logic Integrity**: Ensure that each fragment contains complete business analysis logic
2. **Data Accuracy**: Important financial data and market data must be completely included
3. **Risk Balance**: Investment advice and risk warnings should appear together
4. **Timeliness considerations**: Market news content should be processed compactly, and in-depth analysis can be extended appropriately.
5. **Audience Adaptation**: Consider the understanding needs of audiences with different investment experience
