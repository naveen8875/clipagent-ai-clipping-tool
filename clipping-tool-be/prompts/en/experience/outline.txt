# Experience sharing video outline extraction Prompt

## Input format
You will receive a JSON input:

{
"text": "{Video transcribed text snippet}"
}

- The `text` field is a fragment of the video transcribed content;
- This content has been manually proofread to ensure basic semantic coherence;
- Please only use the input content as a source of information, without any external expansion, common sense reasoning or logical supplementation.

## Core mission: Pay equal attention to practical value and operability
Your task is to **completely cover** all important experiences and techniques in the input text, while maintaining **moderate refinement** and avoid over-refinement.The goal is to extract **8-15 core topics**, each topic corresponding to **3-8 minutes** of video content.

## Topic extraction principles

### 1. Practical value priority principle
- **Operation first**: Prioritize the extraction of experience sharing with clear operating steps and practical skills.
- **Problem-solving orientation**: Focus on experience that can solve specific problems and meet actual needs
- **Avoid empty talk**: Skip purely theoretical discussions and focus on specific experiences that can be replicated and practiced.

### 2. Principle of empirical integrity
- **Method System**: Ensure that each experience sharing contains a complete method system (background - method - effect - precautions)
- **Detail-rich**: Focus on experience descriptions containing specific details and operational points
- **Result Verification**: Highlight experience sharing including actual results and successful cases

### 3. Principle of audience applicability
- **Reasonable threshold**: Prioritize the extraction of experience content suitable for the ability level of the target audience
- **Clear Scenario**: Focus on experiences with clear applicable scenarios and usage conditions
- **Replicability**: Highlight empirical methods that are universally applicable and easy to replicate

### 4. Value Hierarchy Principle
- **Core Skills**: Prioritize the extraction of key skills and experience sharing of core methods
- **Advanced Techniques**: Focus on advanced techniques to improve efficiency and optimize effects.
- **Pitfall Avoidance Guide**: Highlighting experiences and lessons learned in preventing mistakes and avoiding risks

## Topic merging and splitting strategies

### Merge conditions (the following situations should be merged into one topic):
- Different aspects or levels of the same skill or method
- Interrelated operational steps or process links
- Different solutions or techniques for the same problem
- Different application experiences in similar scenarios

### Split conditions (the following situations should be split into different topics):
- Experience in different skill areas or application scenarios
- Clear distinction between basic skills and advanced skills
- Solutions for different problem types
- Separation of theoretical learning and practical application

## Output format

Please output strictly in the following format without adding other descriptive text:

### Outline:
1. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Subtopic 1: Highlight practicality and operability]
- [Subtopic 2: Highlight practicality and operability]
-…

2. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Subtopic 1]
-…

## Special consideration for experience sharing content

### 1. Operational steps experience:
- Ensure completeness and logical sequence of steps
- Contains key operational points and precautions
- Provide specific time nodes and effect expectations

### 2. Skills and methods experience:
- Clarify the applicable scenarios and usage conditions of the technique
- Contains specific operational details and practical points
- Provide performance evaluation and improvement suggestions

### 3. Problem solving experience:
- Clearly describe the problem identification and analysis process
- Includes the basis for solution selection and implementation steps
- Provide effect verification and subsequent optimization methods

### 4. Experience content on avoiding pitfalls:
- Clarify error identification signs and prevention methods
- Contains remedial measures after errors occur
- Provide coping strategies for similar situations
