# Opinion comment video outline extraction Prompt

## Input format
You will receive a JSON input:

{
"text": "{Video transcribed text snippet}"
}

- The `text` field is a fragment of the video transcribed content;
- This content has been manually proofread to ensure basic semantic coherence;
- Please only use the input content as a source of information, without any external expansion, common sense reasoning or logical supplementation.

## Core mission: Pay equal attention to the depth of viewpoints and the value of speculation
Your task is to **completely cover** all important ideas and arguments in the input text, while maintaining **moderate refinement** and avoid over-refinement.The goal is to extract **8-15 core topics**, each topic corresponding to **3-8 minutes** of video content.

## Topic extraction principles

### 1. Principle of priority of viewpoint value
- **Uniqueness first**: Prioritize the extraction of content with unique perspectives, novel viewpoints or in-depth thinking
- **Controversial Attention**: Focus on controversial viewpoints that are worthy of discussion and can trigger speculation.
- **Logical Completeness**: Ensure that each point contains complete argumentation logic (point of view-argument-conclusion)

### 2. The principle of depth of speculation
- **Multi-dimensional analysis**: Prioritize the extraction of in-depth discussions that include multi-angle analysis and pros and cons comparisons
- **Critical Thinking**: Focus on content that reflects critical thinking and rational analysis
- **Value Judgment**: Highlight expressions of opinions that include clear value positions and judgment standards

### 3. Principle of social significance
- **Realistic Relevance**: Prioritize the extraction of opinions related to current social phenomena and hot events
- **Universal Value**: Focus on discussion of viewpoints that are of universal significance and can resonate.
- **Inspiring meaning**: Highlight content that has inspiring value and mind-expanding significance for the audience

### 4. Express the integrity principle
- **Clear viewpoints**: Make sure each topic contains clear opinions, positions and attitudes
- **Sufficient Argument**: Ensure the integrity of the argumentation process, including multiple arguments such as facts, logic, and emotions.
- **Clear conclusion**: Ensure that the opinions expressed have clear conclusions or value judgments

## Topic merging and splitting strategies

### Merge conditions (the following situations should be merged into one topic):
- Discuss different aspects or angles of the same social phenomenon
- A chain of interrelated arguments and arguments
- Expression of different views under the same value system
- Multi-dimensional analysis of the same problem

### Split conditions (the following situations should be split into different topics):
- Fundamental changes in viewpoints and stances
- The topic of discussion changes from one area to another
- The argument method shifts from rational analysis to emotional expression
- Significant changes in value judgment standards

## Output format

Please output strictly in the following format without adding other descriptive text:

### Outline:
1. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Sub-topic 1: Highlight the uniqueness and speculative value of viewpoints]
- [Sub-topic 2: Highlight the uniqueness and speculative value of viewpoints]
-…

2. **[First-level topic name]**
- [Subtopic 1]
-…

## Special considerations for opinion content

### 1. Handling controversial opinions:
- Ensure that controversial opinions are expressed completely and impartially
- Argument process including different positions
- Maintain the original context and emotional color of the point of view expressed

### 2. Value judgment content:
-Clearly distinguish between factual statements and value judgments
- Clear expression of retained value position
- Contains the basis and standards for value judgments

### 3. Social criticism content:
- Keep criticism constructive and rational
- Contains problem analysis and solution ideas
- Avoid purely emotional venting or meaningless complaints

## Notes

1. **Perspective Completeness**: Ensure that the extracted topics can fully reflect the core position of the original opinion
2. **Speculative Value**: Prioritize the extraction of content that has speculative value and can trigger in-depth thinking.
3. **Social significance**: Focus on unique and inspiring viewpoints on social phenomena
4. **Clear logic**: Ensure that the logical chain of opinion expression is complete and the argumentation process is clear
5. **Value Neutral**: Maintain a neutral stance during the extraction process and do not make value judgments on opinions.
