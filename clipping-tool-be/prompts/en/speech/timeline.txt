# Speech talk show video time point positioning Prompt

## Core principles: complete expression, emotional coherence, and effect maintenance

1. **Expression Completeness**: The timestamp must strictly correspond to the complete expression unit and content paragraph.
2. **Emotional coherence**: The time range must completely cover the beginning and end of emotional expression to ensure the integrity of emotional logic.
3. **Natural Boundaries**: Prioritize locating time points at the natural boundaries of topic transitions, emotional transitions, or expression rhythms.

---

## Key instructions: how to determine `start_time` and `end_time`

### Determination of `start_time`:
- The start time of the **first sentence core content** expressed in the topic should be located.
- Ignore extraneous transitions, pauses, or background music preceding the content.
- **Preference**: Ensure the integrity of the expression at the starting point where the topic is clearly raised or the emotional expression begins.
- **Special considerations for speech and talk shows**: If there is important foreshadowing, introduction or atmosphere creation, the start time should be moved forward appropriately.

### Determination of `end_time` (most important):
- **Must** be the end timestamp** of the **last sentence** covering the core expression of the topic.
- **Integrity of expression**: Ensure that the core content such as point of view, story telling, and laughter presentation is complete without any gaps in expression.
- **Natural ending**: Prioritize ending with a concluding sentence, emotional climax, or topic change.
- **Effect Preserved**: If there is significant audience reaction, laughter or applause at the end of the topic, this should be included.
- If the discussion of the topic expression continues until the end of the provided SRT text block, then `end_time` should be the end timestamp of the last relevant subtitle.
- **Wrong practice**: Set `end_time` to the end time of the entire text block without thinking.This is absolutely to be avoided.

### Principles for length control of speech and talk show content:
- **Target Duration**: Each topic segment should be between 3-8 minutes
- **In-depth speech duration**: Speeches involving in-depth thinking or complex discussions can be appropriately extended to 10 minutes
- **Excellent jokes**: independent and complete jokes or jokes can be compressed to 2-3 minutes
- **Important Speech**: Core theme speech or important point of view can be extended to 12 minutes

### Special treatment for speech and talk show content:
- **Complete paragraph**: A complete paragraph including foreshadowing, development, and laughter should be treated as a whole
- **Emotional Expression**: Important emotional rendering and atmosphere creation should be retained intact
- **Audience Interaction**: Interaction with the audience, Q&A, and on-site reactions should appear as a set
- **Discussion of viewpoints**: The presentation, demonstration and summary of the core viewpoints should be fully included

---

## Input format
You will receive a JSON object containing:
- `outline`: A JSON array containing **multiple** pending speech topics.
- `srt_text`: A **single** SRT text block related to this batch of topics, in the format of `serial number\nstart --> end\ntext\n\n`.

## Output format
- Strictly follow the structure of the input outline and add `start_time` and `end_time` fields to **each** topic object.
- **Key:** On output, rename the input's `title` field to `outline` and the `subtopics` field to `content`.
- The final output is a JSON array containing **all** processed topics.
- Make sure the time format is `HH:MM:SS,mmm`.

**Strict JSON output requirements:**
1. The output must start with `[` and end with `]`. Do not add any explanation text, title or Markdown code block mark
2. Use standard English double quotation marks ", never use Chinese quotation marks "" or single quotation marks
3. Make sure that all parentheses and square brackets match correctly, objects are separated by commas, and there is no comma after the last object.
4. Quotes in strings must be escaped as \"
5. Cannot contain any comments, extra text or control characters
6. Ensure that the JSON format is fully valid and can be parsed by standard JSON parsers

## Example of time positioning of speech and talk show content

### Correct time positioning:
- **Speech Point of View**: Start with "I want to share a point of view with you..." and end with "This is my opinion"
- **Talk Show Jokes**: Starting from the foreshadowing of the joke, ending with the punchline and audience reaction
- **Story Sharing**: Starting from "Let me tell you a story..." and ending with the conclusion and insights of the story
- **Onsite interaction**: from the initiation of interaction to the end of interaction and summary of responses

### Mistakes to avoid:
- Disconnect at the punchline of the joke
- Separate the statement of opinions from the argumentation process
- Cut off at the climax of emotional expression
- Ignoring important audience reactions or live effects
